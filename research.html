Research

Research interests

My research centers on battery materials and data-driven experimental workflows, with an emphasis on connecting synthesis, characterization, and electrochemical performance.

Battery Materials

Designing compositionally complex cathode materials for sodium-ion and lithium-ion batteries, with attention to phase stability, redox behavior, and cycling performance.

Battery Safety

Understanding materials-level factors that influence safe and reliable electrochemical energy storage.

Lab Automation

Building automated workflows for synthesis planning, precursor calculation, data processing, and high-throughput materials characterization.

AI-Driven Materials Discovery

Applying machine learning and data-driven methods to accelerate composition screening, experiment planning, and structure-property analysis.
